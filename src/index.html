Leftie Aubé

Author

Leftie Aubé is a horror writer who lives near Québec city. She's a supporting member of the Horror Writers Association. Her flash fiction, CAN'T STAND IT ANYMORE, was published in the collection WHEREVER WE ROAM, where it was chosen as an honorable mention. Her short story IF WE GO, WE DON'T COME BACK was chosen as an honorable mention in a writing contest and will be published in 2025. She's the host Aubé's Writing Podcast, where she shares her writing and publishing journey with vulnerability and positivity to help other writers navigate their own journey. You can find her on Instagram, Threads, Twitter and TikTok @leftieaube.

She is represented by Ameerah Holliday at Serendipity Literary.
